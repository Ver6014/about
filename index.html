About Me

I make projection art, take photos, experiment with sound, and sometimes build things. I have two cats, one named Joker and the other Dr. Pepper. Most of my work is just me having fun and experimenting. I do not like to pay for software so most of the tools I use are free. Right now, I am working on school mostly, but in my free time, I am having fun with video feedback loops, datamoshing, metasynth, and Bryce 3D. I am also learning how to make VST/Audio Unit plug-ins.
